The two developers manage the operations and develop the application(s)

The initial start was bumpy, but going live the developer had become a Kubernetes enthusiast

The Kubernetes hosting is by far:

  • More secure
  • Better performing
  • More complete

Cost of operations went up 10%, but cost of ownership went down 80% (two FTE)

Client felt in control again and still does!
